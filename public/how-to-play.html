How to Play

Getting Started

Create an account by clicking "Join Now" on the homepage. Verify your email and log in to your account.

Game Mechanics

  • Bidding: Use AdPixel Tokens (APT) to bid for territories on the digital map. The highest bid wins the territory.
  • Territory Claiming: Once you win a bid, claim your territory and start developing it.
  • Pixel Grouping: Group pixels for added protection and strategic advantages.

Game Modes

  • Weekly Wipe: A fast-paced mode with weekly map resets.
  • Monthly Wipe: A mode with monthly map resets for longer-term strategies.
  • Fixed Board: A stable mode with no map resets, perfect for long-term investments.

Tokens and Tokenomics

AdPixel Tokens (APT) are used for all in-game transactions. Earn APT through gameplay, staking, and participating in governance.
